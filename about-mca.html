MCA

ABOUT

The Khallikote Autonomous College introduced 3 years Master of Computer Applications (MCA) under self-financing mode in 1997. The MCA course has been approved by AICTE and affiliated to Berhampur University. MCA course has been designed to generate quality System Analyst and software professionals. Specialized topics offered are Computer Communication and Distributed Database, Image Processing, Object Oriented Programming, and natural language processing (Artificial Intelligence), Parallel Computing, Internet and Multimedia information system Design, Cryptography, Theory of Automation, Compiler Design, Design Algorithm and Analysis. The department lays stress on the practical and application based aspects through laboratories, seminars, group discussions, viva-voce and project work, keeping pace with the growth in computer technology. Students are given scope to conduct experiments on innovative ideas. With the need of the changing technology the department updates the standard of its syllabus and staff orientation through continuous feedback interactions and exchange of ideas with students and motivational seminars.


Now the Khallikote Autonomous College is upgraded to Khallikote Unitary University from 1st August 2021.

ADMINISTRATION

The Registrar of the University acts as the Director of the MCA department. The MCA department is administered by HOD of Mathematics and other faculty members of mathematics.
